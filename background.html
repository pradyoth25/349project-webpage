Background

Data Set

The data we used was gathered from ponpare.jp, a Japanese coupon website, over the course of a year. Over that year, 22,873 users' transactions were tracked, with 19,413 possible coupons to purchase. The sheer size of the dataset directed our approach. We also had to translate some of the columns from Japanese to Enlish to make sure we could understand all of the data and the affect it had on our results
